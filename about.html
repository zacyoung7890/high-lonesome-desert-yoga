About Chelsea

Chelsea Young is a private yoga instructor from Arizona who finds inspiration in nature, cats, and traveling. Her sessions are peaceful and uplifting, yet thoughtfully challenging—expect gentle strength, mindful breathwork, and a feel‑good workout. And every now and again, you might see a curious cat pop up during a session.

Training & Focus

  • Vinyasa, Hatha, Yin, and Restorative
  • Breathwork (Pranayama) & Meditation
  • Mobility, fascia release, and stress resilience
  • Personalized progressions for all levels

Philosophy

Light and bright—honoring natural cycles of sun and moon. Chelsea’s sessions emphasize mindful transitions, gentle strength, and the spacious quality of desert landscapes.
